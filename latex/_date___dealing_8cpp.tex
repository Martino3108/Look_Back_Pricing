\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implementation of date parsing and year-\/fraction computations. 

Implements 30/\+360 EU, 30/\+360 US, and ACT/\+\+ACT ISDA conventions using {\ttfamily std::\+chrono}. The dispatcher {\ttfamily \doxylink{_date___dealing_8cpp_a16d5f3ec21ae41b86d6c1b6368f186f8}{year\+Fraction()}} selects the appropriate convention and returns the fraction of years between two dates.
